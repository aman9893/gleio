Frequently Asked Questions

Our Frequently Asked Questions here.

What is Gleio??

Gleio is a digital solution for hotels & their guests to make their stay experience a much more comfortable one rather than the usual hassle of checking-in and utilizing hotel services.

Is Gleio a free service?

Gleio is a completely free service for the app users can download from the playstore. However the hotels are required to contact us to get registered with us.

Can I book rooms through gleio?

No, you cannot book rooms through gleio. We're currently not booking hotel rooms through the app yet, however once your booking is done you can check into hotels with the app, we do have the booking feature coming soon.

How do I use hotel services with gleio?

Once your checkin request is approved by the hotel, on the checkin date as soon as you collect your keys from reception, you will be able to access Hotel menu. Gleio will automatically take you to the Hotel Dashboard. This will be active till you checkout from the hotel.

I have problem, what do I do?

We're terribly sorry for the inconvenience. Goto the help section in the app & follow the procedure listed there. Or you can contact us at info@gleioapp.com
